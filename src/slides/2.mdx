    <h1>JavaScript has real costs.</h1>
    <ul>
      <li>Parse and execution time blocks rendering</li>
      <li>Hydration failures cause flashes of unstyled content</li>
      <li>Event listeners accumulate and leak</li>
      <li>Behaviour breaks silently when JS fails or is blocked</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="aside">This isn't anti-JavaScript. It's about <em>deliberate</em> JavaScript.</p>
