<div class="entryscreen">
  <div class="content-slide">
    <p class="label">The honest slide</p>
    <h1>Some things still need JavaScript.</h1>
    <ul>
      <li>Server communication and data fetching</li>
      <li>Complex state shared across distant components</li>
      <li>Drag and drop interactions</li>
      <li>Canvas, WebGL, audio</li>
      <li>Focus management in complex widgets</li>
      <li>Anything that needs to know what happened before</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="aside">The goal was never to remove JavaScript. It was to stop reaching for it automatically.</p>
